Meet the Mentors



Meet Mara Hart!

As a University of Virginia student, Mara has taught other students the basics of programming through Python as a CS 111X TA. She is also learning web and mobile app development on the side as well as an Instructor's Assistant for Kode With Klossy, a non-profit that promotes coding literacy in girls. During the fall of 2019, she is studying at Fudan University (复旦大学) in Shanghai, China, learning Chinese and entrepreneurship and finance.

She’s involved in a lot of clubs at school including Capture the Flag which is a cyber security club, BU Hacks which is the club that arranges the yearly hackathon and a club based around everyone's favorite game Mafia Club. Meet Abby Slovick!

Abby is currently studying education policy at Columbia University’s Teachers College. After teaching high school STEM, Abby realized her passion is in writing policy and advocating for all children at a level outside of the classroom. Abby continues to interact with students through Kode With Klossy because she believes that in order to write the best policy, one must understand what is happening at the classroom level. Abby’s educational interests include education history, sociology of education, and quantitative data analysis. Beyond education, Abby loves hiking the outdoors, traveling to new places, and hanging out with cute dogs!!


Meet Tara Tran!

Tara Tran fell in love with coding after teaching high school math for two years through Teach For America and being a Kode With Klossy Summer Instructor, where she taught JavaScript and Ruby. Tara is passionate about expanding more voices into the world of STEAM and is currently living out that mission as the Manager of Recruitment and Instruction for Kode With Klossy. Tara will be attending New York University in the fall in pursuit of a Masters in Computer Science.
